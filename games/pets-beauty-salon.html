How to Play

In Pets Beauty Salon, you'll take care of cute animals and help them look their best!

  1. Select a pet to style from the available options
  2. Clean and groom your pet with various tools
  3. Apply cosmetics, accessories, and outfits
  4. Complete all tasks to finish the makeover
  5. Take a photo of your finished work!

Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the salon tools and create adorable pet styles.
