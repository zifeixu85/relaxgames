How to Play

Match colorful candies to clear levels in this sweet puzzle game!

  1. Swap adjacent candies to create matches of 3 or more
  2. Create special candies by matching 4 or more in a row
  3. Clear all obstacles and reach the target score
  4. Complete each level before running out of moves
  5. Unlock new levels and challenges as you progress

Click or tap on a candy and then on an adjacent candy to swap them and create matches!
