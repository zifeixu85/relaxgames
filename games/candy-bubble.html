How to Play

Dive into a sweet world of colorful candies in this addictive matching puzzle game!

  1. Swap adjacent candies to create matches of 3 or more
  2. Match more than 3 candies to create special power-ups
  3. Use power-ups to clear multiple candies at once
  4. Complete level objectives before running out of moves
  5. Progress through increasingly challenging levels

Use your mouse or touch the screen to swap candies. Plan your moves carefully to create powerful combos!
